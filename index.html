CEO stands for "Chief Executive Officer." It is the highest-ranking executive or leader in a company or organization. The CEO is responsible for making major corporate decisions, managing the company's overall operations, and ensuring the organization's strategic goals and objectives are met. The CEO typically reports to the company's board of directors and may work with other top executives, such as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO), Chief Operating Officer (COO), and Chief Marketing Officer (CMO), to guide the company's direction and ensure its success.
